# Best Practices

> **"Technical skills get you into the profession. But MINDSET and SOFT SKILLS determine how far you go."**

Think of it this way: Technical skills are like a **car engine** — they make the car run. But mindset and soft skills are like the **steering wheel and map** — they determine whether you stay on course and how far you travel. A QA who knows Playwright, Postman, and SQL but lacks mindset and communication is like a sports car without GPS: fast but lost.

This section focuses on the things **no course teaches you** but that make all the difference between an ordinary QA and an outstanding one.
